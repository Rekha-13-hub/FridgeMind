How It Works ?

Simple steps. Smarter living.Get started in minutes and let your fridge work for you.

Step 1

Connect Your Fridge

Quickly set up your fridge by manually entering items or scanning barcodes and receipts. You can add product names, quantities, expiry dates, and even categories so everything is organized from the start. Setup takes just minutes and gives you complete control.

Step 2

Track & Update

Once your fridge is connected, the app keeps track of everything automatically. It monitors what’s in stock, what’s running low, and what’s about to expire. You’ll get gentle reminders and real-time updates, so you never lose track of what’s inside.

Step 3

Get Smart Suggestions

Based on your fridge data, the app generates shopping lists, meal ideas, and usage tips tailored to your needs. Whether it’s a recipe to use up spinach or a reminder to buy milk everything is customized to your household.
